5 Apps Merging Wellness & Music
Music has a huge impact on brain health and in turn, mental and physical health; so it makes sense that rhythm and music is an integral part of wellness routines of all types. It’s easy to think solely of physical health when hearing the term “wellness”, but the definition is evolving as mental health awareness increases. This evolution has led to app developers combining sound and science to create some of the coolest health apps around – not only for “gym rats” and yogis but for those of us needing meditation guidance, anxiety relief, and more.

Read on for 5 of our favorite apps for a healthy mind, body, and spirit…
